SPAS TASK FORCE CONSULTING SDN.BHD.

Welcome to the UM SPAS Task Force Consulting Sdn.Bhd..

SPAS Task Force is a student-led environmental consultancy firm who will bring a new voice to industrial environmental protection all over Malaysia.

Company Background

Founded in 2019, SPAS Task Force Consulting Sdn.Bhd. emerged from the Environmental Impact Assessment course (SIH3005) at Universiti Malaya. Our consultancy firm is driven by a commitment to implement innovative solutions and foster a positive environmental impact while ensuring the highest standards of professionalism and expertise.

Our Team

Our team comprises dynamic and enthusiastic individuals, each bringing unique expertise and skills to the table. From seasoned researchers to aspiring environmentalists, our diverse group collaborates cohesively, ensuring comprehensive and tailored solutions for our clients.

Professional Capabilities

At SPAS Task Force, we specialize in conducting thorough Environmental Impact Assessments (EIA) for various industries, including aquaculture, universities, and more. Our proficiency extends to delivering comprehensive reports and recommendations that align with regulatory standards and promote sustainable practices.

Mission & Vision

Our mission is to provide a conducive work environment for our staff, fostering innovation, collaboration, and continuous learning. Moreover, we are dedicated to resolving our clients' environmental challenges efficiently, ensuring their satisfaction and contributing to society's Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs).

We aim to achieve a harmonious balance between environmental protection and industrial development, striving to be a leading force in promoting sustainable practices for a better tomorrow.
